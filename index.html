Welcome to Jane Doe Research

Jane Doe Research is a non-governmental organization in Osogbo, Nigeria. It was established on 1st February, 1983. The objective for its establishment is to give effect to the world's drive for the much-needed research in Science, Engineering and especially Technology.

Our Vision

To become a world class and world's leading academic research website recognized for its excellence in trasnformative discoveries and innovation.

Our Mission

The mission of Jane Doe Research is to advance wolrd-class research and innovation across the entire world. We provide the world with comprehensive, solution-oriented service and infrastructure to support research, dissemination and translation of discoveries to maximize impact.
